## 1. Introduction
### 1.1 Background and Rationale

The concept of divine intervention in human affairs has been a cornerstone of religious and spiritual traditions throughout history. From ancient scriptures to contemporary testimonies, accounts of divine presence manifest in various forms - miraculous healings, prophetic revelations, answered prayers, and extraordinary synchronicities. Despite the prevalence of such accounts, systematic scientific investigation of these phenomena remains limited.

This research addresses a fundamental gap in our understanding of divine-human interaction by applying rigorous analytical methods to document and analyze what we term “footprints of God” - observable manifestations of divine presence in human history and experience.
