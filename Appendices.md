## Appendix A: Glossary of Divine Terms and Archetypes

| Term | Definition |
|------|------------|
| Divine Presence | The experiential or perceived manifestation of a transcendent or immanent sacred force. |
| Archetype | A symbolic, universal pattern or template that recurs across cultures and religions (e.g., The Savior, The Mother, The Warrior). |
| Avatar | A manifestation or incarnation of God in human or other form, particularly in Hindu traditions. |
| Theophany | A visible or experiential manifestation of a deity to a human being. |
| Sacred Signature | A unique combination of behaviors, energies, and signs that align with divine characteristics. |
| Spiritual Ethnography | Field-based observation and documentation of spiritual or religious behaviors with sensitivity to divine possibility. |
| Anubhava | Direct inner experience or realization, especially of a mystical or divine nature. |
