# Pilot Study: “The Divine Presence: Footprints of God”

## **Pilot Study Focus Area Selection**

**Recommended Pilot Focus: “Divine Interventions in Modern India (1947-2025)”**

*Rationale for Selection:*

- Geographic proximity to researcher base (India)
- Well-documented historical period with accessible records
- Cultural familiarity with local religious traditions and languages
- Living witnesses available for direct testimony
- Manageable 78-year timespan for comprehensive coverage
- Rich interfaith context (Hindu, Islamic, Sikh, Christian, Buddhist traditions)

-----

## **Phase I: Pilot Study Framework Development (Weeks 1-4)**

### **A. Operational Definitions**

**Divine Intervention Categories:**

- Miraculous healings with medical documentation
- Prophetic revelations with verifiable fulfillments
- Divine protection during crises (natural disasters, conflicts)
- Supernatural phenomena witnessed by multiple credible sources
- Answered prayers with documented outcomes
- Divine guidance resulting in significant life changes
- Religious visions and apparitions with lasting impact
- Unexplained phenomena at sacred sites

**Evidence Classification System:**

- **Category A:** Multiple independent witnesses + physical evidence + expert verification
- **Category B:** Multiple witnesses + documentary evidence
- **Category C:** Single credible witness + corroborating circumstances
- **Category D:** Testimonial evidence only (preliminary documentation)

### **B. Geographic Scope Definition**

**Primary Focus Regions:**

- Delhi NCR (political/administrative center)
- Mumbai-Pune corridor (commercial hub)
- Varanasi-Allahabad region (spiritual heartland)
- South India: Chennai-Bangalore-Hyderabad triangle
- Punjab (Sikh religious center)
- Rajasthan (Hindu pilgrimage sites)
- Kerala (Christian and Hindu traditions)
- Northeast states (tribal and Buddhist influences)

### **C. Temporal Framework**

**Decade-by-Decade Analysis:**

- **1947-1957:** Partition period divine interventions
- **1958-1967:** Post-independence spiritual movements
- **1968-1977:** Emergency period and spiritual resistance
- **1978-1987:** Religious revival movements
- **1988-1997:** Globalization impact on spirituality
- **1998-2007:** Digital age religious phenomena
- **2008-2017:** Social media era divine manifestations
- **2018-2025:** Contemporary documented cases
