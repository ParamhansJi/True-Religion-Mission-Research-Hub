## IV. CELESTIAL CONSORTS & DIVINE PAIRS

### ✔ Definition:

The doctrine of Celestial Consorts & Divine Pairs reveals that creation unfolds through sacred duality — the union of Divine Masculine and Divine Feminine forces across all planes of reality. These pairs are not symbolic but ontological truths: they exist as co-eternal expressions of God’s self-manifestation, embedded in cosmic, spiritual, and biological orders.

In True Religion:

Every true creation arises through the sacred pairing of purpose (Divine Will) and expression (Divine Form).

This principle establishes the hierarchical symmetry of existence, rooting all multiplicity in the interplay of original divine pairs.
