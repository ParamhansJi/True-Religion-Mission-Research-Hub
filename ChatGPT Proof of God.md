Definition of Theorem (Academic and Formal)

A theorem is a mathematical or logical statement that has been proven to be true based on previously established axioms, definitions, and other theorems using a valid deductive argument. The term is primarily used in mathematics, logic, and formal systems, but it can also apply to philosophical and theoretical constructs in various sciences.


🔍 Formal Definition (Mathematics & Logic)


A theorem is a proposition or statement that has been logically deduced from axioms and previously established theorems through a formal proof.
