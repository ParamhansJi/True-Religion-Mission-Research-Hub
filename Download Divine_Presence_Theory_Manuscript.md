# The Divine Presence Theory

**Author**: Paramhans Jiddanand  
**Prepared on**: 2025-07-04  

---

**Abstract**

*The Divine Presence Theory* proposes a new paradigm for identifying and discerning divine presence in the contemporary world. Integrating scriptural interpretation, archetypal analysis, phenomenology, digital technology, and empirical observation, this work develops a multidisciplinary framework to assist seekers, scholars, and spiritual communities in recognizing possible manifestations of divinity—whether in individuals, events, or symbols. It offers a sacred yet systematic methodology, resisting both dogmatic certainty and blind skepticism. In doing so, it envisions a future in which sacred recognition becomes both spiritually transformative and ethically grounded.
